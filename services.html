Services I Offer

As a researcher and CFD specialist, I offer various services in the field of computational fluid dynamics and related areas. These services are tailored to meet the needs of industry, academic institutions, and individual clients. Some of the services I provide include:

  • CFD Simulations: Providing high-quality CFD simulations for various applications such as fluid flow, heat transfer, and multiphase flows.
  • Model Development: Developing custom CFD models for specific needs, such as non-equilibrium thermodynamics and complex fluid systems.
  • Consulting: Offering consulting services to help you integrate CFD solutions into your projects, from problem definition to solution interpretation.
  • Training: Conducting workshops and training sessions on CFD modeling, simulation techniques, and the application of CFD in industry.
  • Collaborative Research: Working with academic and industry partners on joint research projects, especially in the areas of multiphase flow and thermodynamics.
