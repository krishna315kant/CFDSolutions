My Research Interests

My primary research focuses on computational fluid dynamics (CFD), multiphase flow modeling, and non-equilibrium thermodynamics. Some of the areas I am currently exploring include:

  • CFD Modeling: Developing advanced models for simulating fluid flows in various industrial and environmental applications.
  • Multiphase Flow: Understanding the dynamics of droplets, bubbles, and solid particles in turbulent flows.
  • Thermodynamics: Exploring non-equilibrium thermodynamic models for applications in heat transfer and combustion.
