About Me

Dr. Krishna Kant is a postdoctoral fellow at IFP Energies Nouvelles, France. He specializes in Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) with expertise in fluid flow modeling, thermodynamic simulations, and advanced numerical techniques.

This portal serves as a gateway to his research, publications, teaching experience, and professional services. Feel free to explore and reach out for discussions, research collaborations, or consulting opportunities.
