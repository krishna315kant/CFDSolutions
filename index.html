About Me

Dr. Krishna Kant is a postdoctoral fellow at IFP Energies Nouvelles, France. Prior to joining IFPEN, he worked at Takasago Fluidic System, Japan as Design Assistant and S and I Engineering Solutions, India as Sr. Development Engineer.

Dr. K. Kant earned his Ph.D. from IIT Hyderabad in 2024, specializing in Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD). His work focuses on fluid flow modeling, thermodynamic simulations, and advanced numerical techniques for multiphase systems.

Dr. K. Kant holds an M. Tech in Fluid and Thermal from IIT Guwahati and a B. Tech in Mechanical Engineering from Lovely Professional University.

As a researcher, he developed numerical methods for multiphase flows, including solver enhancements and theoretical modeling. His research aimed at improving accuracy in droplet dynamics, breakup, and collision modeling across a wide range of conditions.

This portal serves as a gateway to his research, publications, teaching experience, and professional services. Feel free to explore and reach out for discussions, research collaborations, or consulting opportunities.
